About Me

👋 Hey there!

My name is Unnsse Khan and am a Principal Software Engineer living in San Diego, California, USA specializing in highly scalable distributed backend systems on the JVM (Java, Kotlin & Scala).

My career has consisted of providing solutions for a diverse array of vertical industries, including FinTech, Consumer Electronics, PaaS, and IoT, for companies ranging from startups to Fortune 100. Contributed in roles ranging from Lead Software Engineer & Individual Contributor to Primary Developer on distributed (domestic & international) cross-functional teams, handling end-to-end software engineering.

Additionally, trained and mentored numerous software engineering teams in advanced techniques and best practices, along with facilitating their transition to Agile methodology.

Being community-driven and altruistic, I am currently the Co-Organizer of the San Diego Kotlin User Group as a volunteer. Have also been long term active member of the San Diego Java Users Group and Agile Alliance communities.

Am available for consulting and full-time opportunities, both remote or onsite.
